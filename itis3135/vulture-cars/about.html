About Us

Flying is our specialty

Invincible Vultures Flying Cars started out as a crazy idea when Invincible Vulture decided that flying is for everyone. He proposed a world where the average working class family could have their own flying contraption taking them to new heights.

After many late nights of Invincible Vulture drilling the greatness of his plan into my head, we finally got to work. The first iterations of our wings were nothing near perfect. We tried 3d printed models, wings made out of House Depot plywood and even paper mache wings. Though the process was long and unforgiving, our products are now available to the public featuring a variety of wings, rotors and even jet engines.

Built to the highest standard of engineering, our products are guaranteed to give you an unforgettable experience that is safe and sustainable. Flying with our machines is nothing more than a dream come true and we hope that you can enjoy them too.
