Page Design

Home Page

  • Purpose: Welcomes users
  • Simple introduction describing what Ben’s Remodel is
  • Includes pictures of projects
  • May contain a contact form (no validation required)
  • Contains navigation links and a submit form button
  • Form submission may trigger an email (if API available)

About Us Page

  • Purpose: General description of the company
  • Describes who Ben’s Remodel is and what they do
  • Content includes paragraphs and pictures
  • No validation or interactive data entry

Reviews Page

  • Purpose: Leave and view customer reviews
  • Includes form fields for submitting reviews
  • Some validation to reduce fake reviews
  • Buttons: Submit, Clear
  • Data processed upon submission

Previous Projects Page

  • Purpose: Showcase before and after project examples
  • Content includes pictures and descriptions
  • May include dropdown menus or interactive elements
  • No validation required

Project Overview

The goal of this project is to build a website for a remodeling company to attract more customers. The website should have all general information about the work that the company does and anything a potential client might want to know. Upon visiting the website, users should know what services Ben’s Remodeling can provide and be more inclined to hire them than other companies.

The website will include jobs that have already been completed and some examples of what was done. It should also include pricing options and contact information for scheduling quotes or answering questions.
