Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our remodeling services

What types of remodeling services do you offer?

We specialize in kitchen remodeling, bathroom renovations, home additions, and custom projects. Whether you're looking to update a single room or transform your entire home, we can help bring your vision to life.

How long does a typical remodeling project take?

Project timelines vary depending on the scope and complexity of the work. A kitchen remodel typically takes 4-8 weeks, while bathroom renovations usually take 2-4 weeks. We'll provide a detailed timeline during your initial consultation and work to complete your project on schedule.

Do you provide free estimates?

Yes, we offer free consultations and estimates for all potential projects. Contact us to schedule a time to discuss your remodeling needs, and we'll provide a detailed quote based on your specific requirements.

Are you licensed and insured?

Yes, we are fully licensed and insured. We carry comprehensive liability insurance and workers' compensation to protect both our team and your property throughout the project.

What materials do you use?

We use only high-quality materials from trusted suppliers. We work with you to select materials that fit your budget and style preferences, ensuring durability and aesthetic appeal for years to come.

Can I see examples of your previous work?

Absolutely! Visit our Previous Projects page to see before and after photos of completed projects. We're proud of our work and happy to share examples that showcase our craftsmanship and attention to detail.

Do you handle permits and inspections?

Yes, we handle all necessary permits and coordinate inspections as required by local building codes. We ensure all work meets or exceeds local regulations and building standards.

What is your payment schedule?

Payment terms are discussed during the project planning phase. Typically, we require a deposit to begin work, with progress payments throughout the project and final payment upon completion and your satisfaction.
