About Us

We are a group of excited volleyball players that love not only the sport but the community that comes with it. What started out as a few college students playing recreationally has turned into a state-wide organization promoting young athletic individuals that love volleyball.

Summer League

Our summer league runs from May 1st through July 15th. Games are played with teams of six on a standard volleyball court every Saturday at 10 AM. The first two weeks are used for pool play and the season could run longer or shorter depending on the number of teams signed up

Where

All of our events are held at the Downtown Sports Complex located at 123 Main St, Hometown, USA. The complex features state-of-the-art volleyball courts, locker rooms, and a pro shop for all your volleyball needs.

Why Volleyball?

Volleyball is more than just a sport; it's a community. It promotes teamwork, communication, and physical fitness. Whether you're playing competitively or just for fun, volleyball offers something for everyone. Join us and be part of an exciting and supportive community!
